Tips1: use disable archive in impdp
Ref: http://www.dba-oracle.com/t_data_pump_disable_archive_logging.htm
Example:
%MY_ORACLE_ROOT%\bin\impdp \"SYSTEM/%SYS_PWD%@%ORACLE_SID% AS SYSDBA \" logfile=%IG__DUMP_FILENAME%.log dumpfile=%IG__DUMP_FILENAME% schemas=VM1DTA,QUIPOZ,PAXUS,VM1COM,VM1EXC,SESS,CSCSORT  PARFILE=%EXCL_PARA% ENCRYPTION_PASSWORD =%IMP_PWD% LOGTIME=ALL METRICS=Y

DIRECTORY=DATA_PUMP_DIR
SCHEMAS=VM1DTA,QUIPOZ,PAXUS,VM1COM,VM1EXC,SESS,CSCSORT
EXCLUDE=STATISTICS
EXCLUDE=TABLE_STATISTICS
